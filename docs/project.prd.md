# TSNHRBPO: Enterprise HRBPO Super-App PRD (v3.0)

## 1. Vision & Executive Summary
TSNHRBPO is a world-class, **AI-First HRBPO Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)** system. It is designed to modernize and unify the entire spectrum of Human Resource Outsourcing into a single, high-authority digital platform. 

The platform leverages **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash** to provide institutional-grade intelligence across 11 core functional pillars, ensuring that operations are not just managed, but optimized through data-driven automation.

### 1.1 Aesthetic Architecture: Accentuated Minimalism
The system utilizes a high-authority white foundation paired with **Midnight Navy** (for institutional strength) and **Muted Emerald** (for performance signaling). This design handles extreme ERP complexity by emphasizing whitespace and technical typography.

---

## 2. Core Service Pillars (The 11 Dimensions)

### P1: Recruitment & Talent Acquisition
Streamlining the entire hiring lifecycle from institutional sourcing to final executive selection.
1. **Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)**: End-to-end management of client hiring.
2. **Executive Search & Headhunting**: High-end placement for leadership roles.
3. **AI Job Description Engine**: Automated generation of technical JDs.
4. **Candidate Sourcing**: Automated scouting across multiple global platforms.
5. **AI Resume Screening**: Semantic ranking and CV scoring via Gemini 1.5.
6. **Talent Assessments**: Skills-based and psychometric evaluation modules.
7. **Interview Intelligence**: Scheduling, evaluation forms, and sentiment tracking.
8. **Offer Management**: Preparation and issuance of digital offer letters.
9. **Talent Pipelines**: Proactive management of future-ready candidate pools.
10. **Employer Branding**: Support for organizational identity and reputation.

### P2: Onboarding & Employee Lifecycle
Managing the professional journey from induction to exit.
1. **Digital Onboarding**: Automated workflows for new joiners.
2. **Document Verification**: AI-powered identity and credential validation.
3. **Induction Programs**: Interactive orientation and training paths.
4. **Probation Tracking**: Milestone-based monitoring for new employees.
5. **Contract Management**: Lifecycle management of employment legalities.

### P3: Payroll & Global Compensation
Technical financial management with high-authority oversight.
1. **Automated Payroll Processing**: Multi-currency, multi-region disbursement.
2. **Tax & Statutory Compliance**: Localized calculation engines (e.g., EOBI, Social Security).
3. **Payslip Architecture**: Secure, digital disbursement reporting.
4. **Incentive Management**: Advanced bonus and commission calculation.
5. **Expense Reimbursement**: Automated claim processing and audit.
6. **Market Benchmarking**: AI analysis of global and local salary trends.

### P4: Attendance & Workforce Management
Optimizing human capital productivity.
1. **Digital Timesheets**: Geofenced and platform-based attendance.
2. **Leave Workflows**: Multi-level approval systems.
3. **Overtime Logic**: Automated calculation based on labor laws.
4. **Flexi-Schedule Tracking**: Managing remote and hybrid work models.
5. **Workforce Planning**: Shift rotation and allocation optimization.

### P5: Performance & Talent Management
Driving organizational excellence through data.
1. **KPI & Goal Tracking**: Real-time progress monitoring.
2. **Performance Appraisals**: Structured, data-backed review cycles.
3. **360-Degree Feedback**: Multi-level collection of performance data.
4. **Succession Planning**: Identifying the next generation of leadership.
5. **Talent Rating Heatmaps**: Visual distribution of organizational talent.

### P6: Learning & Development (L&D)
Continuous improvement through structured intelligence.
1. **Personalized Learning Paths**: AI-suggested courses based on skill gaps.
2. **Certification Vault**: Tracking professional credentials and expiries.
3. **Leadership Programs**: Targeted training for management growth.
4. **Progress Monitoring**: Tracking the ROI of training initiatives.

### P7: Employee Engagement & Retention
Institutional health and culture management.
1. **AI Pulse Checks**: Sentiment analysis and satisfaction surveys.
2. **Engagement Analytics**: Reporting on organizational cultural health.
3. **Recognition Registry**: Formalized reward and appreciation programs.
4. **Attrition Prediction**: Identifying "at-risk" talent through behavioral data.

### 🏗️ Enterprise SaaS Architecture (v5.0)

TSNHRBPO is modeled after billionaire-scale platforms (Workday, SAP SuccessFactors), utilizing a high-performance **6-Layer Architecture**:

### 1. Presentation Layer (Frontend)
*   **Tech**: Next.js 15, Tailwind v4, Framer Motion.
*   **Zones**: Public Marketing, Client/Employee Portals, Admin Command Center.

### 2. API Gateway Layer (Orchestration)
*   **Tech**: Node.js/Express, GraphQL.
*   **Roles**: Authentication, Rate Limiting, Request Routing to Microservices.

### 3. Microservices Layer (Functional)
*   **Services**: Recruitment, Payroll, Attendance, Performance, Learning, AI Hub.

### 4. AI Intelligence Layer (The Brain)
*   **Tech**: **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash (1.2M Context)**.
*   **Capabilities**: Resume Scoring, Sentiment Analysis, Attrition Prediction, AI JD Drafting.

### 5. Data Layer (Persistence)
*   **Tech**: PostgreSQL (Vector-enabled), Redis (Caching).
*   **Scale**: Architected for 120-200 domain-specific tables.

### 6. Infrastructure Layer (Deployment)
*   **Cloud**: AWS / Vercel (Frontend) / Render (Backend).
*   **Ops**: Docker, Load Balancers, Autoscaling.

## 💎 Design Aesthetic: Accentuated Professional Minimalism

The interface must handle the extreme density of **250+ screens** without overwhelming the user.
*   **Canvas**: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for maximum clarity.
*   **Typography**: Playfair Display (Headers - Authority) | Inter (Body - Precision).
*   **Accents**: 
    - **Midnight Navy**: Navigation, Institutional headers, Primary Actions.
    - **Muted Emerald**: Success states, Growth indicators, Performance metrics.
*   **Architecture**: Bento-grid layouts, thin slab borders (0.5px Slate), and soft glassmorphism for overlays.
